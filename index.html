The thesis

Travel does not fail because one app breaks. It fails because nothing understands the whole movement.

Flights, hotels, ground transport, meetings, weather, traffic, local instability, infrastructure delays, and decision pressure all collide in real time. GÖ.AI is designed to read the trip as a system — not a collection of disconnected bookings.
