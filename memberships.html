FAQ

Membership questions.

Are these final public prices?

No. Founding 100 pricing is early access pricing. Future public pricing is shown to make the Founding 100 advantage clear.

Can I pay monthly?

Yes. Monthly pricing is available at a premium, while annual pricing offers the strongest value.

Is Executive Protection included?

G-KLÜB includes security coordination and integration pathways. Actual protective services are subject to availability, location, legality, and separate operational requirements.

Why join early?

Founding Members help shape the membership experience and receive preferred pricing for the first 12 months after launch.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Yes. Members may upgrade as new mobility, concierge, and executive-access capabilities become available.

Are memberships available internationally?

GÖ.AI is being designed around global mobility. Certain services may vary by region, provider availability, and local regulations.

Will membership benefits expand over time?

Yes. Founding Members receive access to future integrations, mobility partnerships, and premium coordination features as the platform evolves.

What makes GÖ.AI different from a booking platform?

Booking is only the beginning. GÖ.AI focuses on continuity, coordination, and mobility intelligence before, during, and after movement.
